Tomato Garlic Pasta

Description

This delicious made from scratch pasta will blow your mind and taste buds away!

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • olive oil
  • 3-4 servings of pasta noodles

Steps

  1. Chop the tomatoes roughly, the smaller you cut them, the quicker the cooking process will be.
  2. Mince the garlic cloves as small as possible.
  3. Start boiling your pasta for 10 to 11 minutes
  4. On a hot pan, cook down the tomatoes and garlic with plenty of olive oil
  5. When tomatoes are rendered down and have decreased in size by more than half, the pasta should be done
  6. Add the pasta into the sauce and stir until everything is well mixed and you are done!
